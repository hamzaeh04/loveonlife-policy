Love On Life – Account Deletion Request

At Love On Life, we respect your privacy and provide users with the ability to request deletion of their account and associated personal data.

How to Request Account Deletion

To request deletion of your Love On Life account, please send an email from the email address associated with your account to:

loveonlifefoundation@gmail.com

Requests sent from the registered account email help us verify ownership and process your request securely.

Data That Will Be Deleted

  • Profile information
  • User account information
  • User-generated content associated with the account

Data Retention

Certain records may be retained for up to 30 days after a deletion request for:

  • Security and fraud prevention
  • Legal and regulatory compliance
  • System integrity and dispute resolution

After the retention period expires, any retained information will be permanently deleted.
